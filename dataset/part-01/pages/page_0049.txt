July 10, 1947

San Marcos, Texas
[unclear: other]

I wish to acknowledge receipt of your Western Union telegram
dated July 7, 1947. Your interest in making this information available
to me is greatly appreciated.

Inasmuch as the contents of your telegram appear to be of
interest to the War Department only, I have taken the liberty of
making the information furnished by you available to that Department.

Sincerely yours,

John Edgar Hoover
Director

cc - San Antonio
NOTE: The telegram referred to "flying discs."
