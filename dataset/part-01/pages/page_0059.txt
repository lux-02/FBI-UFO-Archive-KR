Office Memorandum - UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT
TO: Director, FBI
FROM: SAC, Butte
SUBJECT: FLYING DISCS

[REDACTED] of Idaho Daily Statesman, Boise, Idaho, telephonically contacted the Butte Office and asked if the FBI was checking on the flying discs reported to have been seen by many citizens. The writer informed [REDACTED] that this office was not making an investigation and inquired as to whether he had contacted Army and Navy officials. He said that he was inquiring of these agencies.
