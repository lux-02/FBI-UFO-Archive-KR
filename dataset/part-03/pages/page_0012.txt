7/12/47 Newsday
COUNTY

Coming His Way
Merrick-Out all day yesterday
looking for a house to rent but
no luck. I got so disgusted I ended
up in a tavern and got lit. When
I got home and went in the house
I SAW FLYING SAUCERS IN
AIR!
-No Foolin.
