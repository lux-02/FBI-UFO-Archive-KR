Our most common skin color is a little darker
than yours, although we have skin colors which match
yours do all over the Earth. Usually, people would
look "tan" to you. We are NOT heliotrope, as has
been suggested!
Concerning our language, we generally speak
two tongues and we are fairly familiar with yours.
In Korendor itself we speak the native language,
Korendian. It is similar in many ways to your
languages since it, like yours, was originally taken
from Galingua, one of the two universal tongues. The
other is Solex Mal, which is more familiar to you,
since it is used by your local Solar System planets.
It is seldom heard now in our sector of space, as
Galingua has replaced it almost entirely.
Unlike your own, however, the Korendian
language is very rhythmic, and has many pleasant
and beautiful sounds, such as "g" or "z". Some
sharper sounds like "c" and "k" are minimized. It is a
beautiful language which is very musical and pleasant
to hear.
Perhaps you would be surprised to learn that
ancient Latin was liberally taken from Galingua.
There are many similarities in the two languages, and
modern English has roots on other worlds.
In concluding my message to you, may I say to
people of Earth: open up your minds and your hearts
to the world around you. There is so much to be
learned, so much to be discovered. Don't spend
precious hours and days in bigotry and ignorance,
and in senseless fighting and killing. To do so is
truly a crime against nature and humanity.
At this stage where you have the very answer
almost in your grasp, how irrational and barbaric it
is to spend futile hours warring with your brothers!
As an old saying on your world puts it: "As you make
your bed, so shall you lie in it." The decision does not
seem difficult. Here are the alternatives: a world
living in harmony, justice, security and abundance
for all, or a world continuing to reap bloody harvests
that grow from the seeds of mistrust. If left to their
own devices, the majority of mankind will forge onward
to a destruction and annihilation, oblivious to their
actions. They are short-sighted; they have no regard
for the future.
It is you New Age people, dedicated to the welfare
of all mankind, and having the vision and understanding
of the ways to build the better world of tomorrow,
who must lead the way. You have the future in YOUR
hands, if you will but shoulder your responsibilities.
The Flying Saucer Movement, as you call it, belongs
to you. Support it in every way possible. Help it to
grow until it is influential throughout your world. Go
out into the world and find the misguided, find the lost,
and reclaim them. We are behind you all the way. As I
said many times before, we will take two steps for every
one that you take to help yourselves.
The time has come for nations to "bury the hatchet",
to clasp hands, and plunge into the future together -
in co-operation toward mutual goals. Look to the
heavens and realize that you are not in bleak atomic
desolation, but in the great greatness that you can
achieve if you will work for it. Choose the right path
and you will qualify to join with us in our journeys among
the stars.
