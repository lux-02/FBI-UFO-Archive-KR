When I first saw the object diving, its speed was very slow. It was much slower than
a plane could dive. On the 45 degree downward slant, it slowed even more. When it
moved across the road, the speed was about 15 miles per hour. During the upward slant,
the speed was much faster than the downward slant and it must have been increasing in
size because as it drew away, we could see it just as plainly and about the same size
as when observed up close.
