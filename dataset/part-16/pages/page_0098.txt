'Flying Saucer' Lands
In Police Trophy Room
A city resident called police Mon-
day morning and reported a flying
saucer in his yard. It developed that
he was well soused, police reported,
but at least he had something tangible
to offer in evidence.
When scoutcar officers W. L. Brown
and W. B. Cole arrived, the man was
wearily stroking an object about the
size of a softball. Now the police
squad room has a new trophy—a piece
of an old meteorite.

Oklahoma City Times
July 10, 1950
