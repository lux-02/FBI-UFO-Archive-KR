#1 QUALITY OF ACTIVITY PATTERN: The classifications listed below of
the types of objects are very approximate and doubtful, due to short
period of observation in most reports. A sphere may appear as a flat
disc when seen at great distance; a disc may appear cigar-shaped if
seen only edgewise, or vice-versa; A disc may resemble a sphere if
seen 'fullface' and rotating, especially on a hazy day.

Other than something unknown and/or strangely maneuvering was seen,

conditions. Daytime objects are classified as well as possible by

from entire country, except continued weakness in NW. The Northeast is
most notable in its increase from very slight activity to heaviest
activity of all areas.
