SUBJECT: Visual Sightings of Unidentified Flying Objects
over Oak Ridge, Tennessee

TO : District Commander
8th District OSI (IG)
Maxwell AFB, Ala.

1. At 0830 16 October 1950, Mr. George Rathman, Security
Division, Atomic Energy Commission, Oak Ridge, Tennessee, notified
the undersigned that visual sightings of an unidentified flying
object had been made by several persons at Oak Ridge. Accompanied by
W. B. Gray, SAC Knoxville CIC, the undersigned proceeded to Oak Ridge.
