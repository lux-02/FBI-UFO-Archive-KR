Reurlet 2-10-49.

A review of your referenced letter reflects that Dr. Lincoln La
Paz, Meteorologist, indicated to Mr. Paul Ryan, Office of Special Investiga-
tions, Roswell, New Mexico, that two men whom he interviewed at Kenna, New
Mexico apparently had some knowledge that the "unidentified aerial
phenomena" was not a meteorite.

It is believed desirable that you endeavor to ascertain the
identity of these two individuals at Kenna, New Mexico and to interview
them for any information which they may be able to furnish relative to
this matter.
