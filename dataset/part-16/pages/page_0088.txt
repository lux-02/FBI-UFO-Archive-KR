November 9, 1950

On May 25, 1949, about 4:00 a.m., a captive balloon escaped.
The balloon was of rubberized nylon fabric, 18' long and 7' in
diameter. The balloon was found to have landed on the outskirts of
Loudon, Tennessee, before 5:00 a.m. on the same date.
