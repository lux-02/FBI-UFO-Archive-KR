January 30, 1975

Dr. J. Allen Hynek
Director
Center for UFO Studies
Post Office Box 11
Northfield, Illinois 60093

Dear Dr. Hynek:

Your article entitled "The UFO Mystery," which
you prepared for the FBI Law Enforcement Bulletin, has been
published in the February, 1975, edition and begins on page 16.
I am sure that the information contained in this interesting article
will be of value to law enforcement officers throughout the country,
and my associates and I want to take this opportunity to express
our appreciation for your cooperation.

Several advance copies of the Bulletin are enclosed,
and I shall be glad to see that additional ones are sent to you if
desired.

Sincerely yours,
C. M. Kelley

Clarence M. Kelley
Director
