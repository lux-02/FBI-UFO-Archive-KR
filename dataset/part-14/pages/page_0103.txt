WASHINGTON D.C.
ZIP CODE 20535

"Flying Saucers"

LA HABRA, CA
THURSDAY, OCTOBER
LA HABRA

DEAR SIR,
I WOULD LIKE TO KNOW IF THE
UFO SIGHTINGS IN THE SKY ARE
TRUE OR FALSE. THERE HAVE BEEN
NUMEROUS SIGHTINGS RIGHT HERE
IN THE STATE OF CALIFORNIA. WHAT
KIND OF BEINGS OR PEOPLE FLY
THESE FAST MOVING MACHINES? I
THAT THESE SIGHTINGS ACROSS
UNITED STATES FROM THE ATLANTIC
COAST TO THE PACIFIC COAST AND
IN THE GULF OF MEXICO SHOULD
BE REPORTED TO PRESIDENT NIXON.
MAYBE HE KNOWS WHY THEY ARE
SCARING CERTAIN PEOPLE IN THIS
COUNTRY. THE F.B.I. SHOULD BE
ON THE LOOKOUT FOR MORE OF THESE
SIGHTINGS RIGHT AROUND WASHINGTON
[unclear: other]
YOUR ANSWER. THANK YOU.
OCT 30 1975
YOURS SINCERELY
